4. QUESTION FLOW FOR AI GENERATION
For each policy add a guided question flow of 5 to 8 questions before generating the document.
The answers should be stored and passed into the AI generator.

Suggested question sets:

NET ZERO / CLIMATE CHANGE POLICY
- What is the company name?
- What country or countries does the company operate in?
- What is the target year for net zero?
- Does the company currently measure Scope 1 emissions?
- Does the company currently measure Scope 2 emissions?
- Does the company want to mention Scope 3 emissions?
- What are the main reduction actions? (energy efficiency / renewable electricity / travel reduction / supplier engagement)
- Who is responsible for reviewing this policy?

ENERGY MANAGEMENT POLICY
- What are the main sources of energy use?
- Does the company monitor electricity monthly?
- Does the company use gas, fuels, or both?
- Are there energy reduction targets?
- Is renewable electricity used?
- Who owns energy data?

WASTE MANAGEMENT POLICY
- What waste streams exist? (general / recycling / hazardous / electronic)
- Does the business track waste volumes?
- Are there recycling targets?
- Are licensed waste contractors used?
- Who is responsible for waste management?

CODE OF CONDUCT
- Should this apply to employees only or employees plus suppliers?
- Should it include anti-bribery language?
- Should it include human rights language?
- Should it include environmental responsibility language?
- How should concerns be reported?
- Who owns and reviews the policy?

ESG / SUSTAINABILITY POLICY
- Which ESG topics are in scope?
- Does the company report ESG metrics?
- Does it want to mention carbon reduction?
- Does it want to mention diversity and inclusion?
- Does it want to mention supplier standards?
- Who reviews the policy?

BUSINESS CONTINUITY POLICY
- What are the main business risks?
- Does the company depend on one site or multiple sites?
- Are cloud systems critical to operations?
- Is there already a disaster recovery process?
- Who is responsible during an incident?
- How often should continuity planning be reviewed?

CONFLICTS OF INTEREST POLICY
- Who does the policy apply to?
- What types of conflicts should be disclosed?
- Should gifts and hospitality be referenced?
- Who receives disclosures?
- What is the escalation process?

EMPLOYEE WELLBEING POLICY
- What wellbeing topics should be included? (mental health / stress / workload / support)
- Does the company offer flexible working?
- Are managers expected to support wellbeing check-ins?
- Is there an employee assistance process?
- Who owns the policy?

TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT POLICY
- Does the company require annual training plans?
- Are compliance and ESG topics included?
- Should managers hold development reviews?
- Is training tracked centrally?
- Who approves training priorities?

DATA RETENTION & CLASSIFICATION POLICY
- What data categories exist? (employee / customer / supplier / financial / operational)
- Does the company want classification levels? (public / internal / confidential)
- Are retention periods already defined?
- Who approves deletion or disposal?
- Who owns the policy?
